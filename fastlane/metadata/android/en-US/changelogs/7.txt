v1.6.0 (M3: Multi-Wallet):
- Multiple wallets: create, import, and switch between separate mnemonic and raw-key wallets
- HD sub-accounts derived from a parent mnemonic
- Transaction history and balance cache scoped per wallet
- PIN now mandatory while a wallet exists; existing users prompted once on upgrade
- Key material moved from EncryptedSharedPreferences to Room, encrypted with Android Keystore AES-256-GCM
- CSV transaction export
- Infinite-scroll transaction list (Paging 3)
- Guards against deleting the active wallet, deleting a parent wallet with sub-accounts, and importing a duplicate mnemonic or raw key
