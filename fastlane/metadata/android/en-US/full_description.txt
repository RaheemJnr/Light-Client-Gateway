Pocket Node is the only Android CKB wallet that runs a real light client directly on your device. No remote servers. No middlemen. Full sovereignty over your Nervos blockchain interactions.

Unlike typical mobile wallets that depend on third-party servers, Pocket Node embeds a Rust-based CKB light client as a native library, giving you direct access to the Nervos network.

Features:
- Embedded CKB light client running locally via JNI
- BIP39 mnemonic seed phrase generation and import
- Biometric and PIN authentication
- Send and receive CKB with QR code scanning
- Real-time balance and transaction history
- Full node sync status monitoring
- Mainnet and testnet support
- No accounts, no emails, no KYC

Security:
- Private keys never leave your device
- Keys stored in Android EncryptedSharedPreferences
- All blockchain queries run locally through the embedded light client
- No analytics or tracking
- Fully open source

Pocket Node is funded by a CKB Community DAO grant and is free and open source software.
