Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

A complete CKB wallet that puts you in full control — built for privacy, security, and simplicity.

Embedded Light Client

Run a real CKB light node on your phone. Every transaction and balance is verified locally — no third-party servers involved.

BIP39 Seed Phrase

Standard 12-word recovery phrase with BIP44 key derivation. Your seed is encrypted with your device's hardware security module.

Biometric & PIN

Protect your wallet with fingerprint, face recognition, or a 6-digit PIN. Required every time you open the app or send a transaction.

Send & Receive CKB

Send CKB with real-time fee estimation. Scan QR codes to fill addresses instantly. View your full transaction history.

Node Status Dashboard

Monitor your light client in real time — tip block height, connected peers, sync progress, and live logs from the embedded node.

Mainnet & Testnet

Switch between CKB mainnet and Pudge testnet at any time. Perfect for developers who want to experiment first.

How It Works

Up and running in minutes

No accounts to create, no emails to verify, no KYC. Just open the app and go.

1

Install

Download Pocket Node from Google Play. Under 15 MB.

2

Create Wallet

Generate a 12-word seed phrase and set up biometric or PIN protection.

3

Sync

The embedded light client connects to the Nervos P2P network and syncs automatically.

4

Transact

Send, receive, and verify CKB transactions — all locally on your device.

Security

Hardware-grade protection

Your private keys are encrypted by your device's Trusted Execution Environment or StrongBox hardware security module.

  • Keys never leave your device — encrypted with AES-256-GCM in TEE/StrongBox
  • No analytics, no tracking — zero data collected or transmitted to any server
  • PIN with per-device salt — lockout protection after failed attempts
  • Local verification only — every block header verified by the embedded light client
  • Fully open source — audit the code yourself
